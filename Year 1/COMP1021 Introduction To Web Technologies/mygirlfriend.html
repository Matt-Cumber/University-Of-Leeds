Meeting Lucy

I met Lucy on the 25th of July 2016 during NCS. From the first time we met we became inseperable almost instantly. We spent all of summer 2016 together doing all sorts activites together ranging from 6 mile hikes, abseiling and shooting arrows with bows, to gardening, selling cakes and packing bags in Iceland. It was one of the best summers all thanks to her. We carried on messaging until our NCS graduation on the 1st of September where it could have been our final time seeing each other. However, we both decided to meet after at a future date in a local Costa. We met several times before we finally became offcial.
