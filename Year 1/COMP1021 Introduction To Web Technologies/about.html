Me

This is me. I am 18 years old and stand at 6 feet 2 inches tall. I was born in Essex and lived there for about 6 years until I moved to the county of Lincolnshire. It was very different moving from a well populated area to the countryside, but over time I realised I preferred the open fields and animals over the tall buidings and overcrowded streets. A few things about me are I have passed my driving test, I drive a Vauxhall Corsa, I graduated NCS, I'm a student at the University of Leeds, I have never broken a bone, I was tongue tied as a child, my favourite film is Saving Private Ryan, my favourite meal is a chicken and pasta bake, and my favourite colour is white despite many people arguing white is not a colour it is a shade.

Family

I live with my father and my two sisters and have done for about the past 8 years. My mother moved back to Essex and is now divorced to my father. Nearly all my family live in Essex including grandparents, aunties, uncles, cousins and second-cousins, but I do have a grandmother and aunt and uncle who live in Lincolnshire closeby. I used to visit Essex every year for 2 weeks in the summer whilst my mother lived with me. After that, I used to visit once every month sometimes missing a few. Now, as a Univeristy student, I visit when I can.
